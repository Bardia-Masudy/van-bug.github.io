    Sponsorship¶

    VanBUG is possible through the work of volunteers, however we welcome support in order to bring in outside speakers and host other significant events.

    If you’re interested in sponsoring an event (a night, a term, or a year) please contact us at dev@vanbug.org

    VanBUG welcomes bioinformatics-related companies to present short (maximum 10 minutes) presentations before VanBUG meetings. To discuss this with the VanBUG team, please contact us at: dev@vanbug.org.
